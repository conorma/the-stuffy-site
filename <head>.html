Here you can learn all about the different stuffies!

Name Stuffie Age Description
Klaus Stuffie Polar bear stuffie 193 Klaus is the grandfather of many stuffies and the mayor of Stuffietopia.
Franky Stuffie Dog stuffie 190 Franky is Klaus' younger brother and is addicted to candy. He eats so much candy that it's a wonder he doesn't get diabetes!
Christopher Stuffie Brown bear stuffie 16 Christopher is Klaus' oldest grandchild. He is extremely strong and athletic. He also has a passion for mini golf and adventures.
Sally Stuffie Seal stuffie 15 Sally is one of Klaus' favorite grandchildren and Christopher's cousin. She loves swimming and doing things with her grandfather.
Mousey Stuffie Mouse stuffie 15 Mousey has a weakness for cheese and loves skateboarding. He is always ready to try new things. He has a nemesis named Blousey and disappears whenever Blousey is near.
Baby Panda Panda stuffie 16 Baby Panda loves boxing, wrestling, and karate. He also enjoys playing sports, eating, rock climbing, and biking. He is one of Klaus'grandchildren and is remarkably large for his age.
Beep beep Brown bear stuffie 14 Beep beep is Christopher's younger brother who happens to be an unusual shade of gold. He may be only 14 years old, but he has accomplished many things, such as climbing Mount Beden.This bear loves skateboarding, sports, and video games. He is the youngest of his family and also happens to be a billonare. Most of that money comes from his grandfather, Klaus, who is a very rich bear. Beep beep is also a computer whiz.
Pooh Stuffie Brown bear stuffie 15 Pooh is Christopher's younger brother and Beep beep's older brother. He loves playing the saxaphone and cleaning up after everyone. He may be a brown bear stuffie, but he's yellow. (Strange, right?!) For some strange reason, he looks a lot like Winnie-the-Pooh.
Dino Stuffie (pronounced DEE-NO) Tyrannosaurus Rex stuffie 15 is Sally's friend (and cousin!) who loves building and has dreams to be a docter. She is a 15-year old T-rex dino who has two younger brothers. Dino loves wrestling her brothers, playing shawrock, (a game when you throw teeth at rocks) racing, and playing board games.
